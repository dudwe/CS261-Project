    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    What won't my query send?

    This may be due to one of the following reasons:

    • A query has been previously submitted and is awaiting a response from the server.
    • The query contains more than 250 characters.
    • The query is empty.

    Why am I getting an error as the response?

    This may be due to one of the following reasons:

    • You mistyped your query.
    • You've asked for information on a company/sector not present in the FTSE 100
    • The type of information requested is not available.

    What type of information can I request?

    See Example Queries below.

    What companies and sectors are present in the FTSE 100?

    See Favourites modal, by clicking the link at the top of the page in the navigation bar.

    Why can I not record voice input or hear voice output?

    Some browsers do not support voice synthesis or voice output. Check if your browser supports speech.

    What currencies are supported?

    Currently Great British Pounds (GBP), Euros (EUR) and the United States Dollar (USD) are supported.

    Chat Buttons
    send

    Send Query - Submits a query. Warning messages may appear if no query has been entered or previous query has not yet received a response.
    mic

    Voice Input - Press this button to start voice recording. Press again to stop recording.
    volume_off

    Volume Toggle - All responses output a voice response which can be toggled on or off. Press this to mute or unmute voice synthesis.
